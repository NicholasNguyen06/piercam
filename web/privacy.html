Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

What This Service Does

Scripps Pier Visibility Alerts is a free SMS notification service that sends underwater visibility conditions at Scripps Pier in La Jolla, California to opted-in subscribers.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver this service:

  • Phone number — used solely to send you SMS visibility alerts

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, or any other personal information. We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on this website.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used exclusively to send you automated SMS messages about underwater visibility at Scripps Pier. We will never use your number for marketing, promotions, or any unrelated purpose.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with any third parties. The only third-party service involved is Twilio, which processes and delivers the SMS messages on our behalf.

Message Frequency & Rates

You may receive up to 2 messages per day when visibility conditions are checked. Message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Contacting us at nick@nickhough.com

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts.

Data Retention & Deletion

If you opt out, your phone number will be removed from our subscriber list promptly. You may also request deletion of your phone number at any time by contacting us.

Security

Phone numbers are stored securely and access is limited to the service operator. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the service, contact us at:
nick@nickhough.com
